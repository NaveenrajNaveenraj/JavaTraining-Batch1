List of Fashion

Vintage Fashion Style
- Vintage clothing is a generic term for garments originating from a previous era. The phrase is also used in connection with a retail outlet, e.g. in vintage clothing store.
Bohemian Fashion Style
- Bohemian style is a fashion style strongly related to hippie fashion
Chic Fashion Style
- The Chic Style Type is known for wearing monochromatic ensembles and pieces with sleek clean lines
Artsy Fashion Style
- This refers to clothing adopted completely or partially from traditional costumes of different nations
